ABOUT STOCKS

-->Stock Market Function: Stocks are primarily traded on stock exchanges, and their prices can fluctuate based on supply and demand. The stock market allows companies to raise money and investors to buy ownership in companies and potentially earn returns
--> Investor Benefits: As part of the ownership, shareholders may receive dividends and have the potential for capital gains if the value of the stock increases over time
-->Risk and Return: Historically, stocks have outperformed most other investments over the long run, but they also come with higher risk compared to less volatile investments like bonds
--> Regulations: Stock trades are regulated by government entities to protect investors from fraudulent practices and ensure a fair trading environment
